# Sources — dunbars-number

> *Primary sources for the [dunbars-number](../SKILL.md) skill.*

- Dunbar, R. I. M. (1992). "Neocortex size as a constraint on group size in primates." *Journal of Human Evolution*, 22(6), 469–493. The original empirical paper.
- Dunbar, R. I. M. (2010). *How Many Friends Does One Person Need?* Harvard University Press. ISBN 978-0674057166. Accessible treatment with organizational extensions.
- Gladwell, M. (2000). *The Tipping Point.* Little, Brown. ISBN 978-0316346627. Chapter 5: Gore case + Dunbar number in organizational design.
- Dunbar, R. I. M. (1998). "The social brain hypothesis." *Evolutionary Anthropology*, 6(5), 178–190. The social brain hypothesis underpinning the neocortex constraint.
- Hill, R. A., & Dunbar, R. I. M. (2003). "Social network size in humans." *Human Nature*, 14(1), 53–72. Empirical validation of the 150 figure via social network analysis.
- Gore, W. L. (1985). "The lattice organization: A philosophy of enterprise." *Networking Journal*, Spring/Summer. The Gore founding philosophy.
