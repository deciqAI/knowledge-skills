# Sources — mr-market

> *Primary sources for the [mr-market](../SKILL.md) skill.*

- Graham, B. (1949/1973). *The Intelligent Investor.* Harper & Brothers (rev. ed. 1973). The foundational chapter 8. ISBN 978-0060555665.
- Graham, B. & Dodd, D. L. (1934). *Security Analysis.* McGraw-Hill. ISBN 978-0071592536 (2008 reprint). The technical predecessor.
- Buffett, W. E. (1987). "Letter to Shareholders." *Berkshire Hathaway Annual Report*. https://www.berkshirehathaway.com/letters/1987.html
- Buffett, W. E. (2008, October 17). "Buy American. I Am." *The New York Times*. https://www.nytimes.com/2008/10/17/opinion/17buffett.html
- Marks, H. (2011). *The Most Important Thing: Uncommon Sense for the Thoughtful Investor.* Columbia Business School Publishing. ISBN 978-0231153683.
- Cunningham, L. A. (Ed.) (2013). *The Essays of Warren Buffett: Lessons for Corporate America* (4th ed.). The Cunningham Group. ISBN 978-1611634099.
- Pabrai, M. (2007). *The Dhandho Investor.* Wiley. ISBN 978-0470043899.
- Klarman, S. A. (1991). *Margin of Safety.* HarperBusiness. ISBN 978-0887305108. (Out of print; the modern value-investing classic.)
- NVIDIA Corporation (2023–2025). Quarterly and full-year financial results press releases. NVIDIA Newsroom. https://nvidianews.nvidia.com/ (revenue ~$27.0B FY2023, ~$60.9B FY2024, ~$130.5B FY2025; Q4/FY2025 release dated 2025-02-26). Contemporary illustration of quote-vs-value divergence at mega-cap scale.
- Reuters / AP / CNBC (2025, January 27–28). Reporting on the DeepSeek-driven AI-stock selloff and NVIDIA's ~$600B single-day market-value decline (record one-day loss). Illustrates a headline-driven "depressed Mr. Market" episode.
