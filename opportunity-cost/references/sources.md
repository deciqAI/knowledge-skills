# Sources — opportunity-cost

> *Primary sources for the [opportunity-cost](../SKILL.md) skill.*

- Bastiat, F. (1850). *Ce qu'on voit et ce qu'on ne voit pas.* Paris: Guillaumin. The foundational essay; the broken window parable.
- Buchanan, J. M. (1969). *Cost and Choice: An Inquiry in Economic Theory.* Markham. (Liberty Fund reprint 1999, ISBN 978-0865972247.) The modern theoretical treatment.
- von Wieser, F. (1889). *Der natürliche Wert.* Vienna: Hölder. (English: *Natural Value*, Macmillan 1893.) The Austrian-school coinage of the term and concept.
- Henderson, D. R. (2008). "Opportunity Cost." In *The Concise Encyclopedia of Economics*, Liberty Fund. The accessible modern summary.
- Munger, C. T. (2005). *Poor Charlie's Almanack.* Donning Company. ISBN 978-1578645015. Opportunity cost as foundational investment concept.
- Buffett, W. E. (2003). Berkshire Hathaway Chairman's Letter. Available at berkshirehathaway.com.
- Grove, A. S. (1983). *High Output Management.* Random House. ISBN 978-0679762881. Opportunity cost applied to management.
- Hazlitt, H. (1946). *Economics in One Lesson.* Harper & Brothers. ISBN 978-0517548233. A 20th-century re-presentation of Bastiat's argument.
