# Sources — non-consensus-thinking

> *Primary sources for the [non-consensus-thinking](../SKILL.md) skill.*

## Sources

**Primary:**

Howard Marks. *The Most Important Thing: Uncommon Sense for the Thoughtful Investor.* Columbia University Press, 2011.
URL: https://www.oaktreecapital.com/insights/memo (memos freely available; book at Columbia University Press)
Verbatim: "Second-level thinking is deep, complex and convoluted... To achieve superior results, you have to have an edge in either information or analysis, or both. You must think of something that others have missed."

Semmelweis, Ignaz. *Die Ätiologie, der Begriff und die Prophylaxis des Kindbettfiebers.* Pest, Wien und Leipzig: C. A. Hartleben's Verlags-Expedition, 1861.
URL: https://archive.org/details/b21989888
Verbatim (translated): "In the year 1847, in the month of May, I recognized that the cadaveric particles adhering to the hands of the examiners were the cause of the endemic puerperal fever prevailing in the First Obstetrical Clinic." (Chapter I, p. 6, trans. K. Codell Carter, 1983)

John Maynard Keynes. Letter to F.C. Scott, 15 August 1934. King's College Archive, Cambridge.
URL: https://www.keynes.org/papers (institutional archive reference)
Verbatim: "It is a mistake to think one limits one's risks by spreading too much between enterprises about which one knows little and has no reason for special confidence... To carry large units of a few securities about which one feels a great deal of confidence, is not riskier than spreading widely."

**Contemporary (2020–2026):**

Kaplan, J. et al. "Scaling Laws for Neural Language Models." 2020. arXiv:2001.08361.
URL: https://arxiv.org/abs/2001.08361
Note: The empirical basis for the scaling thesis — capability/loss as a predictable function of compute, parameters, and data. The published "law" is what converted a minority intuition into a testable, evidence-backed position (Step 3 of the process).

Nvidia Corporation. Investor relations and quarterly financial results.
URL: https://nvidianews.nvidia.com / https://investor.nvidia.com
Note: Primary source for the FY2024 data-center revenue surge and market-capitalization milestones cited in the 2016–2026 AI example. Use quarterly press releases and 10-K/10-Q filings for exact figures rather than secondary reporting.

*What is NOT cited and why:* Pop-business parables (e.g., "Steve Jobs said..."), motivational contrarian aphorisms, and social-media investment commentary are excluded because they are not primary sources and frequently attribute quotes inaccurately or without sufficient context for verification.
