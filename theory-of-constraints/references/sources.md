# Sources — theory-of-constraints

> *Primary sources for the [theory-of-constraints](../SKILL.md) skill.*

- Goldratt, E. M., & Cox, J. (1984). *The Goal: A Process of Ongoing Improvement.* North River Press. ISBN 978-0884271956. The foundational text.
- Goldratt, E. M. (1990). *What Is This Thing Called Theory of Constraints?* North River Press. ISBN 978-0884271666. Theoretical formalization.
- Goldratt, E. M. (1994). *It's Not Luck.* North River Press. ISBN 978-0884271154. Strategic application; introduces the "thinking processes."
- Goldratt, E. M. (1997). *Critical Chain.* North River Press. ISBN 978-0884271536. Project management application.
- Mabin, V. J., & Balderstone, S. J. (2003). "The performance of the theory of constraints methodology." *International Journal of Operations & Production Management*, 23(6), 568-595. Meta-analysis of implementations.
- Cox, J. F., & Schleier, J. G. (Eds.) (2010). *Theory of Constraints Handbook.* McGraw-Hill. ISBN 978-0071665544. Comprehensive treatment.
- Anderson, D. J. (2010). *Kanban: Successful Evolutionary Change for Your Technology Business.* Blue Hole Press. ISBN 978-0984521401. Software development application.
- Watson, K. J., Blackstone, J. H., & Gardiner, S. C. (2007). "The evolution of a management philosophy: The theory of constraints." *Journal of Operations Management*, 25(2), 387-402.
