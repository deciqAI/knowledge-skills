# Sources — map-is-not-the-territory

> *Primary sources for the [map-is-not-the-territory](../SKILL.md) skill.*

- Korzybski, A. (1933). *Science and Sanity: An Introduction to Non-Aristotelian Systems and General Semantics.* Institute of General Semantics. 5th ed. ISBN 978-0937298015. The original source.
- Bateson, G. (1972). *Steps to an Ecology of Mind.* Ballantine Books. Extended the principle into systems theory and epistemology.
- McNamara, R. (1995). *In Retrospect: The Tragedy and Lessons of Vietnam.* Times Books. ISBN 978-0812925333. Primary source case study on map-territory decoupling in strategic management.
- Hayakawa, S. I. (1949). *Language in Thought and Action.* Harcourt. A key popularizer of Korzybski's general semantics framework.
- Meadows, D. H. (2008). *Thinking in Systems: A Primer.* Chelsea Green. ISBN 978-1603580557. Systems-level treatment of model-reality divergence.
- Taleb, N. N. (2010). *The Black Swan.* 2nd ed. Random House. ISBN 978-0812973815. The map-territory failure mode in statistical and financial models.
