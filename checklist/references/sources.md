# Sources — checklist

> *Primary sources for the [checklist](../SKILL.md) skill.*

- Gawande, A. (2009). *The Checklist Manifesto: How to Get Things Right.* Metropolitan Books. ISBN 978-0805091748.
- Haynes, A. B. et al. (2009). "A Surgical Safety Checklist to Reduce Morbidity and Mortality in a Global Population." *New England Journal of Medicine*, 360(5), 491-499.
- Pronovost, P. et al. (2006). "An intervention to decrease catheter-related bloodstream infections in the ICU." *NEJM*, 355(26), 2725-2732.
- Munger, C. T. (1995). "The Psychology of Human Misjudgment." Harvard Law School lecture. Reprinted in *Poor Charlie's Almanack*.
- Pabrai, M. (2007). *The Dhandho Investor.* Wiley. ISBN 978-0470043899. Includes the investment checklist.
- Boyer, S. P. (2014). *The Checklist Manifesto Comes to Aviation.* National Air and Space Museum reference.
- Reason, J. (1990). *Human Error.* Cambridge University Press. The cognitive foundations.
- Pronovost, P. & Vohr, E. (2010). *Safe Patients, Smart Hospitals.* Hudson Street Press. ISBN 978-1594630613. The Pronovost story.
- Roth, D. L. (1997). *Crazy from the Heat.* Hyperion. The brown M&M rider clause as checklist verification.
