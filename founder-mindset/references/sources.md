# Sources — founder-mindset

> *Primary sources for the [founder-mindset](../SKILL.md) skill.*

- **Graham, Paul.** "Default Alive or Default Dead?" *paulgraham.com*, October 2015. http://paulgraham.com/aord.html **Primary source for the load-bearing financial test**.
- **Graham, Paul.** "Do Things That Don't Scale." *paulgraham.com*, July 2013. http://paulgraham.com/ds.html **Primary source for the unscalable-action discipline**.
- **Graham, Paul.** "Schlep Blindness." *paulgraham.com*, January 2012. http://paulgraham.com/schlep.html **Primary source for the avoidance-failure-mode framework**.
- **Graham, Paul.** "The 18 Mistakes That Kill Startups." *paulgraham.com*, October 2006. http://paulgraham.com/startupmistakes.html
- **Graham, Paul.** "How to Start a Startup." *paulgraham.com*, March 2005. http://paulgraham.com/start.html
- **Y Combinator Portfolio.** Public listing of funded companies and valuations: https://www.ycombinator.com/companies
- **Horowitz, Ben.** *The Hard Thing About Hard Things: Building a Business When There Are No Easy Answers*. HarperBusiness, 2014. **Primary source for founder psychology under pressure**.
- **Hoffman, Reid; Casnocha, Ben; & Yeh, Chris.** *The Alliance: Managing Talent in the Networked Age*. Harvard Business Review Press, 2014. Founder-employee relationship model.
- The popular framing "fake it till you make it" is **not** cited as a source — by this skill's own rule, an aphorism is not evidence. Founder mindset is more precisely "*operational discipline applied to specific cognitive failures founders systematically commit*," not personality posturing.
