# Sources — metacognition

> *Primary sources for the [metacognition](../SKILL.md) skill.*

- **Flavell, John H.** "Metacognition and Cognitive Monitoring: A New Area of Cognitive-Developmental Inquiry." *American Psychologist*, Vol. 34, No. 10 (October 1979), pp. 906–911. **Primary source for the term and concept**. https://doi.org/10.1037/0003-066X.34.10.906
- **Pólya, George.** *How to Solve It: A New Aspect of Mathematical Method*. Princeton University Press, 1945; 2nd ed. 1957. **Primary source for the four-stage operational protocol**.
- **Schoenfeld, Alan H.** *Mathematical Problem Solving*. Academic Press, 1985. **Primary source for empirical demonstration of metacognitive monitoring as the load-bearing skill**.
- **Schoenfeld, Alan H.** "Learning to Think Mathematically: Problem Solving, Metacognition, and Sense-Making in Mathematics." In D. Grouws, ed., *Handbook of Research on Mathematics Teaching and Learning* (Macmillan, 1992), pp. 334–370. **Synthesis of the trainability results**.
- **Dunlosky, John & Metcalfe, Janet.** *Metacognition*. SAGE Publications, 2009. **Modern textbook**.
- **Parasuraman, Raja & Riley, Victor.** "Humans and Automation: Use, Misuse, Disuse, Abuse." *Human Factors*, Vol. 39, No. 2 (1997), pp. 230–253. **Foundational source for automation bias and complacency** — the human-factors mechanism behind under-monitoring a trusted automated aid; directly relevant to over-trusting fluent AI output. https://doi.org/10.1518/001872097778543886
- ***Mata v. Avianca, Inc.***, No. 22-cv-1461 (S.D.N.Y. 2023). **Widely reported 2023 court matter** in which attorneys were sanctioned for filing a brief containing fabricated case citations produced by ChatGPT — a public, documented case of AI fluency defeating verification (a metacognitive-monitoring failure).
- The popular framing "self-awareness" is **not** cited as a source — by this skill's own rule, an aphorism is not evidence. Metacognition is more precisely "*the protocol-driven monitoring loop during reasoning*," not a personality trait of awareness.
