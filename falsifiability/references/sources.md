# Sources — falsifiability

> *Primary sources for the [falsifiability](../SKILL.md) skill.*

## Sources

- Popper, K. R. (1934). *Logik der Forschung.* Vienna: Springer. English: *The Logic of Scientific Discovery* (1959). London: Hutchinson. ISBN 978-0415278447.
- Popper, K. R. (1963). *Conjectures and Refutations: The Growth of Scientific Knowledge.* London: Routledge. ISBN 978-0415285940.
- Eddington, A. S. (1919). "Joint Eclipse Meeting of the Royal Society and the Royal Astronomical Society." *Nature*, 104, 354. The relativity confirmation.
- Kuhn, T. S. (1962). *The Structure of Scientific Revolutions.* University of Chicago Press. ISBN 978-0226458120. The paradigm-shift complement.
- Lakatos, I. (1970). "Falsification and the Methodology of Scientific Research Programmes." in Lakatos & Musgrave (eds.), *Criticism and the Growth of Knowledge*. Cambridge University Press.
- Ries, E. (2011). *The Lean Startup.* Crown Business. ISBN 978-0307887894. The startup application.
- Tetlock, P. E. (2015). *Superforecasting.* Crown. ISBN 978-0804136693. Calibrated falsifiability in prediction.
- Mayo, D. G. (1996). *Error and the Growth of Experimental Knowledge.* University of Chicago Press. The error-statistical framework.
